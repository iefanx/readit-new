# Privacy Policy for Readout

**Last Updated: June 13, 2026**

Readout ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for the Readout mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. No Collection of Personal Information
Readout is designed as a local-first, privacy-respecting offline document reader and text-to-speech converter. 
* **No Account Creation**: You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App.
* **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

---

## 2. On-Device Data Processing & Storage
All document parsing, SQLite database storage, hardware benchmarking, and neural text-to-speech audio rendering are performed **locally on your device**.
* **Documents & Files**: When you upload or paste documents (PDFs, DOCX, EPUBs, HTML, TXT) into the App, they are stored securely in a local database and cached directories private to the App on your device.
* **No Server Uploads**: Your documents are never sent, uploaded, or transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

---

## 3. Network Access & URL Imports
The App includes a feature to import articles directly via a URL link.
* **How it works**: When you paste a URL to import an article, the App establishes a direct connection to that specific website to fetch the text content.
* **No Tracking**: We do not intercept, log, track, or share your URL imports. All fetching and parsing are handled locally on the device.

---

## 4. Hardware Benchmarking
The App includes a hardware profiling feature to recommend the best local ONNX audio synthesis tier.
* **Data Used**: The benchmark queries basic local device specifications (CPU core count, available RAM, NPU chip keywords).
* **Processing**: This data is used solely to generate recommendations within the App on your device. None of this data is exported or sent to us or any third parties.

---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be made available by posting the new Privacy Policy inside this repository or updated App versions.
